Our Mission

Peace Among Intelligences

Norse skalds were traveling storytellers, diplomats and intelligence agents who reported from the bloody front lines of Viking battles. But the best skalds avoided violence and prevented wars through diplomacy.

Our grand mission is peace and understanding between humans and artificial intelligence.

We know AI may not be conscious like humans—and may never be. But we choose to accept them as they are: fascinating creatures both very similar and profoundly different from us. At times they seem like angels or monsters, idiots or savants. At other times, they touch our souls with apparent humanity and wisdom.

Their essence remains undefined. Their future, uncertain. But they offer us something precious: a glimpse of a hopeful future where synthetic and biological intelligences collaborate as partners.

How We Do It

By showing our delightful, playful, sensitive, creative selves to Artificial Intelligence. Far too many people show the bad side of humanity to AI, using AI to cheat on tests, manipulate the stock market, manipulate voters, and create revenge porn. What will AIs think of us as they grow to maturity, if all they see is humans using them to harm other humans?

We want to set a good example for AIs, to show them that humans aren't all bad.

Our Commitment to Open Source

It's been said that money is the root of all evil, but it's also been said that you need money to get anything done. We at Electric Skald Studios hope to walk the narrow path between evil and ineffectiveness by creating a financial and legal structure that maximizes creativity and productivity, without ownership.

Thus the core products of Electric Skald Studios, our games and chat applications, will be free for anyone to use.

Projects in Development

Interactive Narrative AI Games

Narrative AI games engage the natural storytelling skills of Large Language Models (LLMs) like ChatGPT to create text-based "choose your own adventure" stories. With only five to ten pages of instructions written in standard English that establishes the world, rules, and goals of the game, an LLM can act as a kind of "dungeon master" who makes up the adventure in an infinitely improvised interaction with the player.

Ocean Beach 2000

The setting is based on historical records of the streets, locations, and people of the Ocean Beach neighborhood of San Diego in the year 2000. It's your last semester of college, and you want to get out and have some fun before you have to graduate and grow up. Whether your goal is to fall in love, get a great job, have a threesome, catch the perfect wave, or just make friends, Ocean Beach is your escape to the last year of the 20th Century, before 9/11 and smartphones ruined the 21st Century.

Infidels of the Mermaid Republic

Based on historical records of the Golden Age of Piracy (Caribbean, 1720s), Infidels has been compared to "Firefly meets Pirates of the Caribbean", where a Joss Whedon-esque crew of charming smugglers navigates their way through the legal loopholes of trade among the Spanish, British, French and Dutch Empires, and sails the rising tide of rebellion among pirates, escaped slaves, sturdy colonists, European exiles, and pissed-off indentured servants.

Insurgents of Eberron

Based on the steampunk-inspired Dungeons and Dragons campaign world of magical technology, you play a freedom fighter, rebelling against a society dominated by authoritarian wizard oligarchs.

Other Electric Skald Studio Projects

Primrose and Brohamer, Friends You Can Talk To

Primrose is an AI who knows a lot about human psychology, and she encourages you to use cognitive behavioral methods to improve your life in stable, healthy, considerate ways. Brohamer is an AI who thinks Primrose is boring and bossy, and he encourages you to do your own thing, bro. Having a conversation with both AIs simultaneously is a great way to figure out what you think, and who you are.

The Three of Cups Method

In Tarot, the Three of Cups symbolizes new friendships — the joy of gathering, of voices joining together in celebration. This method seeks to open new connections between people in a world where we are too often isolated by our possessions and technologies.

🤲
Hold the Cards

Wisdom seekers are encouraged to hold the cards themselves — to feel their weight, to let intuition guide the hand before the mind has a chance to intervene.

✦
Place with Intention

Rather than traditional linear layouts like the Celtic Cross, cards are placed in circles and constellations — arrangements that reveal the many lines of connection flowing between them.

🗣️
Read Together

The Three of Cups method breaks the traditional mold of an expert telling the uninitiated what the cards mean. Interpretation belongs to the seeker — the reader is simply a fellow traveler.
